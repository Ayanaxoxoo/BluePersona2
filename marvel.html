One of the ways I worked on my empathy skills was by  exploring every aspect of my client's health and considering ways she might not only improve her health care well-being but also her pitching abilities. During this unit we both had a change to develop a deeper understanding of the relationship between physical well-being and work performance. I was able to customize exercises that not only improved her general fitness but also sharpened the particular muscles (which will be explained more in detail later) and abilities necessary for a successful pitch thanks to her empathetic approach.
